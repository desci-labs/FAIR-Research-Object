About FAIR-Reserch-Object (FAIROS)

Tool to calculate the FAIRness of Research Objects, serialized as RO-Crates.

FAIROS is a framework designed to evaluate the FAIRness of Research Objects (ROs), which are complex aggregations of data, software, and metadata. It provides:

  • Two scoring approaches to assess the FAIRness of an RO.
  • An initial implementation of these methods within the FAIROS framework.
  • A clear, explanatory approach to visualize the obtained scores.
The framework was validated on a collection of 165 ROs, highlighting strengths and limitations of different scoring systems.

More information on FAIROS is available here: 10.5281/zenodo.7795727
