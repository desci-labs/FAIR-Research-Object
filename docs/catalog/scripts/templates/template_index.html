FAIR-Research-Object (FAIROs)

Authors: Daniel Garijo and Esteban González.

FAIROs is a tool to calculate the FAIRness of Research Objects, serialized as RO-Crates.

If you use FAIROs, please cite our TPDL publication: FAIROs: Towards FAIR Assessment in Research Objects.
